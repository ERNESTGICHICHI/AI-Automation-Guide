Who We Are

Mwaniki Kariuki & Company, Advocates is a reputable, small-sized legal practice based in the City of Nairobi, Kenya. The firm renders a diverse range of legal services to a wide spectrum of clients in Kenya, the East African region and internationally.

The firm is involved in a wide range of litigation matters which are conducted across the board ranging from tribunals, to lower courts, to the High Court and the Court of Appeal and up to the highest Court in the land — the Supreme Court of Kenya.

Property & Conveyancing

In property matters, the firm is closely involved in major industrial, commercial and residential developments, rendering assorted legal services including financing arrangements, contracts arising therefrom and conveyances to various third parties.

Corporate & Commercial

The firm also handles a significant portfolio of corporate and commercial matters including incorporation of companies, registration of foreign companies and other business organizations, corporate restructuring, joint ventures, and business acquisitions.
