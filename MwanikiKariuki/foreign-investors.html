Investing in Kenya? We Navigate the Legal Landscape for You.

The firm has extensive experience advising foreign investors entering the Kenyan market. Whether you are establishing a business, acquiring property, securing licences or navigating immigration requirements, our team provides clear, practical legal guidance at every step.

Our Services

How We Support Foreign Investors

📜

Law Relating to Investment in Kenya

Comprehensive advisory on the legal framework governing foreign investment including the Investment Promotion Act, special economic zones, and sector-specific regulations.

🏠

Property Acquisition

End-to-end legal support for foreign nationals and companies acquiring land and property in Kenya, including due diligence, title searches and transfer.

📋

Licensing & Approval Procedures

Guidance on all permits, licences and regulatory approvals required to operate a business in Kenya, including sector-specific licences.

🏢

Formation of Business Organisations

Incorporation of companies, registration of foreign company branches, formation of partnerships and joint ventures under Kenyan law.

🌍

Immigration Law Matters

Advisory on work permits, residence permits, dependant passes and citizenship matters for foreign nationals relocating to Kenya.

🤝

Joint Ventures & Partnerships

Structuring and negotiating joint venture agreements and strategic partnerships between foreign and local entities.
