01

Conveyancing & Property Matters

In property matters, the firm is closely involved in major industrial, commercial and residential developments, rendering assorted legal services including financing arrangements, contracts arising therefrom and conveyances to various third parties. The firm has built a strong reputation in this area, handling high-value transactions with precision and attention to detail.

🏢

02

Corporate & Commercial Practice

This includes incorporation of companies, registration of foreign companies and other business organizations, corporate restructuring, joint ventures, acquisitions, and shareholder agreements. The firm advises both local and international clients on the full spectrum of corporate and commercial matters in Kenya.

⚖️

03

Litigation Practice

The firm is involved in a wide range of litigation matters which are conducted across the board ranging from tribunals, to lower courts, to the High Court and the Court of Appeal and up to the highest Court in the land — the Supreme Court of Kenya. Mr. Kariuki is a seasoned litigator with an established track record of securing favourable outcomes for clients in complex disputes.

🤝

04

Arbitration & Alternative Dispute Resolution

The firm provides expert advisory and representation services in arbitration and other ADR mechanisms including mediation and negotiation. ADR is increasingly preferred by commercial clients seeking efficient and confidential resolution of disputes outside the court system.

💡

05

Intellectual Property

The firm assists clients in protecting and enforcing their intellectual property rights in Kenya including trademarks, patents, copyrights, and industrial designs. The firm advises on IP strategy, registration, licensing, and enforcement proceedings.

👨‍👩‍👧

06

Family Law & Succession

The firm handles matrimonial matters including divorce, separation, custody and maintenance. It also advises on estate planning, probate administration, and the distribution of estates under both the Law of Succession Act and customary law.

👷

07

Employment & Industrial Relations

The firm advises employers and employees on employment contracts, termination, redundancy, and workplace disputes. It also provides representation before the Employment and Labour Relations Court and relevant tribunals.

🛡️

08

Personal Injuries & Insurance

The firm represents claimants in personal injury matters arising from road traffic accidents, workplace injuries, and other tortious acts. It also handles insurance disputes including claims handling and policy interpretation.

🌿

09

Environmental & Planning Law

The firm provides advisory services on environmental law compliance, environmental impact assessments, planning approvals, and regulatory matters before the National Environment Management Authority (NEMA) and other bodies.

📊

10

Tax & Revenue Practice

The firm advises clients on tax planning, tax compliance, and represents clients before the Kenya Revenue Authority, Tax Appeals Tribunal and courts in tax disputes.
