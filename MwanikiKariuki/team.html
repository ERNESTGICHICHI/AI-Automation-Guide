Mwaniki Kariuki

Founder & Managing Partner

Mr. Kariuki is an accomplished litigator at the High Court and Court of Appeal levels and also practises in corporate and commercial matters. He is the founder of the firm and was previously working in the firm of Gichuki King'ara and Company Advocates where he was exposed to and practised and handled all manner of commercial work including acting for clients against banks and securing clients' rights including obtaining injunctions in favour of clients.

Mr. Kariuki was admitted as an advocate of the High Court of Kenya on 2nd October, 2008. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Law from the University of Nairobi and a Post Graduate Diploma in Law from the Kenya School of Law.

Qualifications & Admission

Bachelor of Laws (LLB), University of Nairobi · Post Graduate Diploma in Law, Kenya School of Law · Admitted to the High Court of Kenya, 2nd October 2008

Caroline Thuo

Senior Associate

Caroline is a Senior Associate at the firm. Before joining the firm, she was a partner at Hamilton Harrison & Mathews, one of the oldest and most respected law firms in Kenya.

She specialises in commercial and conveyancing matters, bringing extensive experience from one of Kenya's most prestigious legal practices to her role at the firm.

Qualifications

Master of Laws (LLM) in International Trade and Investment Laws, University of Nairobi · Post-Graduate Diploma in Law, Kenya School of Law · Bachelor of Laws (LLB), University of Nairobi
